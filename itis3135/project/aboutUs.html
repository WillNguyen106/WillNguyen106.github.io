Lantana Hoi An Boutique Hotel

Hoi An is a city in Vietnam known for its magnificent Ancient Town, which conserves hundreds of buildings with antique architecture from several centuries ago. This historical city is the perfect destination for tourists from around the world, and adding a lovely hotel like Lantana to the equation ensures a memorable adventure. Lantana Hoi An Boutique Hotel is suitable for guests from all backgrounds with its refreshing style and design, professional and friendly staff, as well as the convenient location near the heart of Hoi An. Lantana promises its guests an enjoyable arrival and a delightful departure. Looking back on the memories of the trip, the guests will think of Lantana with a blissful smile and great satisfaction.

Beautiful style and design are important factors of an elegant boutique hotel. Lantana Hoi An Boutique Hotel follows the Mediterranean style, which brings an enlivening feeling to the guests because of the radiant colors. From the welcome drinks made for the guests to the designs in each room, the interior and exterior of the hotel are adorned with the brilliant shades of blue and green. One glance at Lantana on a moonlit night, those who are passing by will not be able to take their eyes off the star-like beauty of the building. The distinctiveness of Lantana is the vintage decorative telephone in every room, adding the indispensable feature of Hoi An Ancient Town to the modern Mediterranean room's architecture. Lantana presents itself to the world with the look of a box-shaped building full of wonders. The hotel is named after the Lantana flower, and the front of the building is decorated with the same colorful flowers which give the hotel its lively aura. One trip to the hotel and a stranger will become a fan of it just by admiring the charmingly ornamental coat Lantana wears.
