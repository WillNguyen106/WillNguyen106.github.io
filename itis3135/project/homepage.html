LANTANA BOUTIQUE HOI AN HOTEL

Situated in one of the most sought after locations in Hoi An, Lantana Hotel sits on the Doai Riverside, located just 5 minutes walk from the centre of Hoi An Ancient town. A perfect blend of a peaceful location and within a few minutes walk of the Ancient Town,makes for a great position loved by our guests.

Being one of the newest Hotels in Hoi An, Lantana still has the look and feel of a new modern hotel, yet furnished in a warm and welcoming style. A variety of rooms, some with balconies overlooking the river means this is a great spot for couples, families and single travelers alike, wishing to explore Hoi An Ancient Town and surrounding areas.

At Lantana Boutique Hoi An Hotel. we have a wide variety of rooms to suit your needs and budget.

Perfectly located in a quieter part of central Hoi An we can assure you a good nights sleep.

Each of room has a distinct style, with beautifully appointed rooms and suites decorated with peace and comfort in mind.
